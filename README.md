# **Global Video Game Sales Industry Analysis**

### **Objective**
This project explores the evolution of the gaming industry across several decades. By analyzing a massive dataset of over 8,000 global sales records, the dashboard identifies market-leading regions, dominant publishers like Nintendo, and the "Golden Era" of specific gaming consoles.

### **Technical Implementation (Advanced Power BI Skills)**
* **User Experience (UX):** Integrated **Dynamic Report-Page Tooltips** to provide context-aware details without cluttering the main view.
* **Complex Aggregations:** Developed DAX measures to calculate regional market shares, identifying that **North America (NA)** accounts for **49%** of the analyzed market.
* **Trend Visualization:** Created a long-term "Sales Trend" line chart to pinpoint industry growth cycles from 1980 to 2020.

### **Key Insights**
* **Market Powerhouse:** North America leads global consumption (49%), followed by Europe (27%) and Japan (14%).
* **Genre Popularity:** **Action and Sports** genres emerged as the all-time favorites, driving the highest global revenue.
* **Platform Legacy:** The **PS2, X360, and PS3** consoles showed the strongest hardware-software synergy in driving global sales volume.
* **Publisher Dominance:** Historical data highlights **Nintendo’s** massive lead in the Platform and Sports categories.
